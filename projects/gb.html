Gillian Bolton

Description

Gillian Bolton is a Canadian Engineer currently residing in London. I created a brand for her as she was looking to progress her career and felt she needed to stand out amongst competitors. I wanted to create a strong presence that utilised textures of materials commonly used in engineering. After weeks of collaboration we arrived at a logo that was inspired by an abstraction of the thread of a bolt. It uses strong, bold lines and type with the aim of reinforcing the core values of solid engineering.
