Mount Kimbie

Description

Mount Kimbie are an electronic music duo, consisting of Dominic Maker and Kai Campos. They formed in London 2008. They released two EP in 2009 and the following year their debut album "Crooks & Lovers" was released by Hotflush Recordings to much critical acclaim. At the tailend of 2012 and shortly before the release of their follow up album I was approached by Dom through mutual friends and asked to contribute a spec album cover as part of a shortlist of designs for the sleeve. The result was a visual play on the refinement in sound Mount Kimbie have undergone. A photographed and digitally enhanced rocky surface printed onto vinyl wrap with an abstraction of a polished gem waiting to be uncovered beneath.
