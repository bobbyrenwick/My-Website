Shot Magazine

Description

Shot Magazine are an online luxury lifestyle magazine founded in Japan. Due to the trend in Japan for consuming all things British, Shot began to ask British artists and designers to create commisioned pieces once every month. They approached me and asked me to create images based around music, with the view of distributing them to their readers as wallpapers the following month. Focusing on the repetitive nature inherent in almost all genres of music, I created several abstract pieces of work and then altered them to best express the recurrent patterns found within a given song.
