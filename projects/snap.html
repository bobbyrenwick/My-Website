Snap

Description

Snap is the new game for iOS that brings the simple pleasures of the popular childhood card game into the 21st century. Players can take on themselves or up to a maximum of 4 others in the same room. The aim of the game is to hit each circle as it's colour changes to match the backgrounds; the player who gets the highest hit ratio wins the game. If you want to up the ante you can increase the number of circles and if that's still not enough, try and hit them when they're moving!
