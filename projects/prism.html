Prism

Description

Prism is an application design for use by large companies and corporations where filtering information to your numerous employees proves to be a very hard task. It allows the section chiefs or managers to upload their own content or link RSS feeds to existing sources. Then all the employee has to do is log into the application and they will be able to view content tailored to their security clearances and their areas of interest and expertise.
